Delicious, Freshly Made Meals all Day.

You're always sure to have a good meal with us! We make our own soups and salads, and we grill all our own chicken. We get our burgers fresh from Klinder Processing nearby.

We also have daily specials for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. And carry-out service is available!

Home of Manhole Cover-Sized Pancakes

When it comes to breakfast, no one beats Herby's Bar Grill & Cafe and our big-as-manhole-cover pancakes. You always get decent breakfast portions when you sit down to eat with us. Enjoy a laid-back atmosphere for the entire family with us!

Let Us Host Your Next Event or Party! We also have a community banquet hall available for your family gatherings and events, complete with onsite catering services.
