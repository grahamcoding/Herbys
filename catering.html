In-Catering and Events

Catering and Hosting Services for Parties and Events

Looking for space to hold a party, gathering, or other festive event? Call Herby's Bar Grill & Cafe for our hall rental and catering services. We have a community banquet hall attached to us that is available for you to rent. We have a speaker system available for bands and DJ services.

We are licensed to be open on Sundays for your event too!

Supporting Our Community

We are proud members of the Carlos Lions Club and a proud sponsor of the Carlos Cougars baseball team. We also support our local Carlos Fire Department. And to help our patrons, we also know American Sign Language to make your visit with us as comfortable as possible.

Our Hall Is Perfect for Your Next Event: Birthdays, Anniversaries, Bridal showers, Baby showers, Weddings, Quinceañeras and more!

A few more details: Our general deposit goes toward your rental fee. We can take reservations for small and large gatherings. We can do next-day services for small gatherings of 20 people. We need one week of advance notice for a large gathering of 200. We can handle all your in-catering needs, and we also have limited out-catering services available. We do not do any alcohol catering.
